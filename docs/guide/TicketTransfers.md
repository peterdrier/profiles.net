# Transferring your ticket

Can't make it, or bought a ticket for a friend under your own name? You can hand
a ticket you're holding to someone else, right from the app. You start it, our
ticketing team finishes it, and both of you get an email when it's done.

## Before you start

- The person you're giving the ticket to needs to be **signed up in the app**.
  If they're not yet, they can make an account in a minute — then you'll be able
  to find them.
- You can only pass on a ticket that's **valid and matched to you**. If your
  ticket isn't showing up as yours, it's usually because the email on the ticket
  isn't on your profile yet — add it under
  **Profile → Emails** and it'll match. (See [Tickets](Tickets.md) for more on
  matching.)

## How to do it

Go to **Tickets → Transfers** (the `/Tickets/Transfers` page). You'll see a short
wizard:

1. **Pick the ticket** you want to pass on, from the list of tickets you're
   holding.
2. **Pick the person** you're giving it to. (It can't be yourself.)
3. **Check the summary** — it shows the ticket and the person's name and email so
   you can make sure it's the right one. You can add a short note about why if you
   like.
4. **Send the request.**

That's your part done. You'll see a message that the request is in, and our
ticketing team takes it from there.

## What happens next

- Your request goes to the ticketing team. They do the actual swap with the
  ticket seller and mark it complete.
- **Both you and the person receiving it get an email** letting you know it went
  through.
- Once it's done, the ticket shows up as theirs (and stops showing as yours) the
  next time the app syncs with the ticket seller — usually within a few minutes.

## Changing your mind

While a transfer is still **waiting to be processed**, you can cancel it yourself
on the same **Tickets → Transfers** page — just hit cancel next to the pending
request. Once the ticketing team has completed it, it's done and can't be undone
from the app (you'd have to arrange a fresh transfer back).

## Quick answers

| If you're wondering… | Here's the answer |
|---|---|
| The person I want to give it to isn't in the app | Ask them to sign up first — then they'll show up when you pick a recipient |
| My ticket isn't in the list | It's probably matched to a different email. Add that email under Profile → Emails and it'll appear |
| How long does it take? | The ticketing team processes it by hand, so it's not instant — you'll get an email when it's done |
| Can I take it back? | Only while it's still pending (cancel it yourself). After it's completed, you'd need to arrange a new transfer the other way |
| Can I transfer to someone outside the org? | They need a Humans account to receive it — signing up is quick and free |
