# The in-app AI helper

See the round **help button** in the bottom-right corner of the app (it's got a
question mark on it)? Tap it and you'll get two choices: **report a problem**, or
**talk with the AI helper** — a friendly chat assistant that can answer questions
about the app and your account.

Think of it as someone who has read this whole guide and knows your account, who
you can ask things in plain words like *"how do I sign up for a shift?"* or
*"why don't I have a ticket showing?"*. It's completely **optional** — you never
have to use it, and everything in the app works without it.

## What it's good for

- **How do I…?** questions about using the app.
- Finding the right page or the right person to ask.
- Sorting out the small stuff — *"my email isn't matching my ticket"*, *"where do
  I change my notification settings?"*
- Helping you **report a problem**: if something's actually broken, it can write
  up the report for you — but **you** get the final say and press send.

## What it can't do

- It **doesn't change anything on its own.** It can suggest and help you fill
  things in, but you're always the one who clicks the button. It won't sign you
  up, transfer your ticket, or edit your profile behind your back.
- It's only there when you're **signed in**. Signed-out visitors don't see it.
- It's not a person. It's usually right, but if something matters, double-check
  with your coordinator or [humans@nobodies.team](mailto:humans@nobodies.team).

## Is it private?

Worth knowing, in plain terms:

- Your chats are **saved to your own history** so you can look back at them. The
  chat panel has a link to your past conversations.
- Organizers can review conversations to keep the helper working well and to keep
  everyone safe — so treat it like a message to the org, not a private diary.
- **Don't paste passwords or anything secret** into it. It never needs them.
- Chats are **automatically deleted after a while**, so they don't pile up
  forever.
- There's an **"AI Terms"** link right under where you type, which spells out
  what's sent and how it's handled.

## Quick answers

| If you're wondering… | Here's the answer |
|---|---|
| Where is it? | The round help button, bottom-right of any page once you're signed in |
| Do I have to use it? | No — it's optional, and the app works fine without it |
| Will it do things for me? | No. It can tee things up, but you press the button |
| Can it see my stuff? | It knows your account so it can help (your teams, shifts, tickets, to-dos). It can't see your passwords |
| It gave me a wrong answer | It happens — check with your coordinator or email [humans@nobodies.team](mailto:humans@nobodies.team) |
| I'd rather just report a bug | Same help button → "report a problem" fills out an issue for the team |
